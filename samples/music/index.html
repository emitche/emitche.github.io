Elizabeth's Music

Well, some of it...

My name is Elizabeth and I don't like sharing much about myself. But... I thought of something I could share more freely and not be scared— music!

I am an avid music listener. Here are some of my favorites.

Ingrid Michaelson
A quirky artist who loves her work. "Blood Brothers" might be my favorite song by this artist.
Keiko Matsui
Keiko Matsui mixes jazz, classical, and a variety of experimental forms. The Piano is my favorite album by this artist.
Linkin Park
A solid alternative metal band who has reinvented itself multiple times over. The band's desire for experimentation makes discovering each of their albums a delight.
P.O.D.
A rock band whose Christian-inspired lyrics tend to be positive and uplifting. This band is unafraid to mix in different music styles. Murdered Love is my favorite album by this artist.
ASIWYFA
And So I Watch You From Afar is an instrumental rock band from Northern Ireland whose upbeat songs are pretty awesome.
MONO
MONO's experimental rock is a mix between classical and rock. A gem of a band from Japan. I love the soft abrasion of their sound.
Sara Bareilles
Beautiful artist with a resilient spirit. Love her lyrics. "Gravity" is my favorite song by Sara Bareilles.
Lauryn Hill
Her straight-forward, real music is a rarity in the music industry.
India.Arie
Positive down-to-earth music and a beautiful voice. My favorite neo soul artist.
Death Cab for Cutie
With excellent lyrics and wonderful musicianship, this indie rock band is a new favorite.
The xx
Good lyrics, good music.

I've also been listening to a lot of different music lately. Here are some current artists and bands I have enjoyed:

  • Explosions in the Sky
  • Stars
  • The Postal Service

In the past year I've discovered that I really enjoy experimental/instrumental rock. The musicians do incredible things with their instruments. Consequently I've developed a greater appreciation for rock music, which it turns out I love though I didn't actually realize it.

I believe that music, just as with other art forms, has the potential to connect people in beautiful ways. Musicians and songwriters have a great responsibility to produce meaningful, life-changing art. Some people create art without thinking about their responsibility to create something meaningful. I tend not to listen to those artists. There's too much music in the world not to seek out what's worth one's time and ear.
