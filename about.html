About

Hi! I'm Elizabeth Mitchell.

I grew up in Detroit, MI and attended school in western Massachusetts. At Williams College, I received a B.A. in American Studies. My degree helped me explore a variety of topics including education, history, literature, and communities in the U.S., which has helped me learn to connect with a variety of people.

I came back to Detroit after college to make a difference. I've learned that the best impact you can make is loving what you do, doing it well, and sharing those skills and talents with others.

I love building web experiences and am a learner of web technologies. I've been a hobbyist for many years and decided in spring 2013 to focus on web development as a career.

I'm also a poet. I enjoy writing and reading great poetry and literature.

In terms of career experience, I have over 5 years of experience doing print design, writing professionally, and editing. I've served as a website administrator for over three years.

I'm a big supporter of nonprofits and the work they do, specifically arts and youth development organizations. I love being a mentor.

In all aspects of my life, I enjoy being a lifelong learner. I enthusiastically support the communities I belong to and the people I have a chance to connect with. My love for people naturally spills over into my work. I love helping individuals achieve their goals. And I love using my skills and knowledge to help make that a possibility.
