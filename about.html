About

Hi. I'm Elizabeth Mitchell.

I'm a web developer and writer.

My long-term goal as a technologist has been to use technology to support positive causes.

I'm a big supporter of youth development nonprofits, specially those that increase literacy and engage kids in the arts.

For a long time I wanted to make an impact. I've learned that the best impact you can make is loving what you do, doing it well, and sharing those skills and talents with others.
