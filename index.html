About

Hi! I'm Elizabeth Mitchell. I grew up in Detroit, MI. I attended school in western Massachusetts at Williams College and received a B.A. in American Studies. My degree helped me explore a variety of topics including education, history, literature, and communities in the U.S.

I came back to Detroit after college to make a positive impact and have had the opportunity to work for a few nonprofits and connect with many more. I'm a big supporter of nonprofits and the work they do, specifically arts and youth development organizations.

I have 5 years of experience in print design, writing, and editing. I have 3 years of experience as a web administrator, setting up sites and maintaining the content. I've recently developed stronger skills in front-end development through two long-term training experiences. I enjoy learning good web development practices.

I'm a poet. I enjoy writing and reading great poetry and literature.

In all aspects of my life, I enjoy being a lifelong learner. I enthusiastically support the communities I belong to and the people I have a chance to connect with. My love for people naturally spills over into my work. I love helping individuals achieve their goals. And I love using my skills and knowledge to help make that a possibility.
